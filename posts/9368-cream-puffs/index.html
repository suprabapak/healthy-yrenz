Cream Puffs

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 158 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 409 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients :

  • 1/2 c. bread flour, sifted before measuring
  • 1/2 c. milk or water
  • 1/4 c. butter
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs

Directions :

  • Heat the milk or water.
  • Add the butter.
  • Bring these ingredients to the boiling point.
  • Add the sifted flour and salt. Cook and stir the batter until it leaves the sides of the pan and forms a ball.
  • Remove it from the heat.
  • Beat in the eggs, one at a time.
  • Be careful to beat 1 egg until well blended before adding the other.
  • Important!
  • Place spoonfuls of batter into 2-inch rounds on a greased tin.
  • Allow 2 inches between the puffs.
  • Bake in a 400u00b0 oven for 1/2 hour. When the puffs are cool, cut a gash in the side of each and fill with whipped cream, custard or chicken salad.
